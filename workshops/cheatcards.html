Mission Cards

// 12 missions · pocket reference

▸ HOW TO USE THESE

Print 2-sided · A4 landscape · 6 sheets. Cut on the dashed lines = 12 A5 cards. Keep one in your pocket during each mission.

⏱️ Each mission = 90 minutes · 👤 kids 10+ · 🤖 1 robot per 2-3 kids

⚡ First time? 5 quick steps

Tap each step as you go. Saved in your browser.

  1. Charge your robots overnight (or 1+ hour) so they're ready to go.
  2. Print a few Agent Journals + a Cheat Card for the mission.
  3. Set up: 1 robot per 2-3 kids, 1 tablet per group, Bluetooth on.
  4. Open Field Manual on the laptop, pick Mission 1.
  5. Open Robot App on tablets, tap Connect, mission begins!

❓ Common Questions

How many kids per robot?

2-3 kids per robot is the sweet spot. Pairs work great too (driver + observer rotate roles).

What if Bluetooth doesn't connect?

99% of the time it's the tablet, not the robot. Toggle Bluetooth off and on. Restart the app. Use Chrome or Edge (Web Bluetooth required).

Can I skip missions or change the order?

Most missions can run alone. Mission 1 (Wake The Robot) is essential first. Mission 10 needs 5 + 6 done. Mission 12 (Final) wants 1-6 minimum.

What if a kid loses focus?

Use an energizer game! Open the Energizer Drills page. Match: bored = wake-up · wild = calm-down · stuck = brain game.

Do I need 12 robots for 12 kids?

No. 1 robot per 2-3 kids. So 4-6 robots for 12 kids. Only Mission 11 (Robot Duels) needs 2+ robots paired.

How long to prepare a session?

15-20 min. Charge robots overnight. Print the cheat card + booklet pages. Set up the floor (cones, tape). The cheat card lists exact requirements.

Can older kids (12+) do these?

Yes! They go faster. Give them harder challenges first or combine 2 missions in one session. Mission 10 (algorithms) is great for 12+.

What if a mission goes over time?

Cut the wrap-up. The 90-min timing is a guide. Kids hate being rushed mid-build. Stop at the next natural break and continue next session.
